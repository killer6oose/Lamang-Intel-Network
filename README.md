# Lamang Intelligence Network

**[lamangintel.net](https://lamangintel.net)** - Community-maintained reference tools for Gray Zone Warfare operators.

Unofficial fan project. Not affiliated with or endorsed by MADFINGER Games.

---

## What's on the site

### Vulture Tracking
A live weekly tracker for Vulture's two active COPs (Combat Outposts) on Lamang Island. Vulture rotates every Monday at 12:00 UTC and the tracker reflects the current confirmed locations with a live countdown to the next reset. Past weeks are logged so you can see recent rotation history. Each location card links directly to the relevant spot on the community tac map.

### Ammo Chart
Ballistics reference covering 13 calibers and 67 individual rounds. Columns include penetration power, damage, armor damage %, muzzle velocity, effective distance, recoil modifier, and the vendor and rank required to purchase each round. Designed for quick at-a-glance comparisons when deciding what to run.

### Boss Intel
Profiles for 12 tracked bosses and vendors with all known possible operating zones listed per target. Each boss entry shows every grid reference and zone type they can appear in - useful for planning a hunt or just knowing whether to expect a boss in a given area.

### Weapon Blueprints
A tracker for collectible weapon skin blueprints. Lists every blueprint in the game, where it can be found, and lets you mark which ones you have already located. State persists in your browser.

### Loadout Builder
An interactive PMC loadout planner built around a slot-based character doll. Select equipment across every gear category, see a live weight breakdown, and share builds via URL.

**Weapons covered:** 32 weapons across primary and sidearm slots with full attachment trees.

**Attachments:** 435 individual attachments across 15 categories (muzzles, optics, stocks, grips, magazines, handguards, and more). Each attachment shows vendor availability filtered by your configured vendor ranks.

**Gear slots:**
- 41 headwear options (helmets, caps, balaclavas, and more)
- 34 body armor and plate carriers
- 16 tactical rigs with configurable slot loadouts
- 10 belts with per-slot item assignment

**Consumables and medical:** 22 consumables, 31 medical items, and 9 containers assignable to pocket, rig, and belt slots.

**Keys:** 103 keys tracked with weight data.

Builds are shareable - the 'Save Build' button encodes the full loadout into a URL you can copy and send to squadmates.

## Contributing

Data corrections and bug reports are welcome via GitHub Issues. Use the issue templates - they are structured by category (weapons, attachments, gear, medical, containers/keys) and auto-apply the right labels to help triage quickly.

If you want to report an error in a specific item's stats, vendor level, or availability, the correction templates will walk you through the format. The more specific the correction and the better the source cited, the faster it can be verified and applied.

---

## Data sources

Item data is sourced from the [GZW Community Wiki](https://gray-zone-warfare.fandom.com) and verified against in-game observation. Stats reflect patch 0.4 (Spearhead) where possible - earlier data may not yet be fully updated. Vendor and rank data in particular was significantly overhauled in 0.4 and some entries may still reflect pre-0.4 values.
