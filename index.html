// About GZW Guides
Community Intel
for Lamang Operators

GZW Guides is a community-maintained reference hub for Gray Zone Warfare. Every tool here is built to answer the questions you'd otherwise need to alt-tab out of the game to find - where is Vulture this week, which ammo punches through armor, what are the possible spawn zones for a given boss.

The Vulture Tracking updates every Monday with confirmed vendor and boss locations, with a live countdown to the next weekly reset. The Ammo Chart covers penetration power, damage, armor damage %, and muzzle velocity for every caliber in GZW - inspired by the kind of structured ballistics data Tarkov players expect. Boss Intel documents all known possible locations for each tracked target.

For deeper lore, item descriptions, quest walkthroughs and more, the GZW Fandom Wiki is the most comprehensive community knowledge base available. Weapon and item links throughout this site point directly to wiki pages.
